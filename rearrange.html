About Me

Sam O. Afolabi, a 27-year-old Nigerian writer, and Publishing consultant was born in Kaduna and raised in Ibadan. His first collection of poems appears in notable places including the country school curriculum for senior secondary school and also bagged two publishing deals in the United Kingdom. He lives in Lagos currently working on his new novel.
